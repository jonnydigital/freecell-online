# Solitaire Network Editorial Calendar

*12-week rolling publication schedule — Q2 2026 (April 6 – June 26)*

**Target cadence**: 16 articles/week across 4 sites
**Cadence breakdown**: 4 hub articles + 4 FreeCell spoke + 4 Klondike spoke + 4 Spider spoke
**Format**: weekly sprints Monday–Friday. Mondays: `solitaire-content-pipeline` gap analysis. Tuesday–Thursday: `solitaire-content-writer` drafts 3-5/day. Friday: human review + publish.
**Total planned**: 192 articles (48 per site × 4 sites)

---

## Editorial Desks (Bylines)

| Desk | Focus | Voice |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Editorial Team** | Masthead, round-ups, opinion, seasonal | Warm, authoritative |
| **The Strategy Desk** | Game theory, tactics, decision frameworks | Analytical, prescriptive |
| **The History Desk** | Origins, Microsoft era, international variants | Narrative, archival |
| **The Rules Desk** | Rules, teaching, beginner onboarding | Plain, patient |
| **The Research Desk** | Simulations, win rates, data journalism | Rigorous, source-backed |

## Site Roles

- **solitairestack.com** — network hub. Cross-game content, history, research, taxonomy, linkbait.
- **playfreecellonline.com** — deepest FreeCell authority. Deal numbers, supermoves, solvability, variants.
- **playklondikeonline.com** — Klondike + Microsoft-era authority. Draw 1/3, Vegas, Yukon.
- **playspidersolitaireonline.com** — Spider authority. 1/2/4-suit, winnability, column tactics.

## Internal Linking Rules

- Every article links to **2-3 existing pages** (cluster connection).
- Every spoke article links **up to the hub** at least once.
- Every hub article links **down to at least one spoke game page**.
- Pillars receive links from all cluster members in their 6-week window.
