### Pyramid layout — descoped to backlog

Confirmed the bug at `solitairestack.com/pyramid`: the page renders as 7 vertical columns (1-7 cards each) instead of a triangular pyramid. Root cause is in the engine adapter at `src/app/(main)/pyramid/PyramidGamePage.tsx` — pyramid rows are coerced into the `cascades: Card[][]` shape that `GenericCascadeBoard.tsx` renders as a horizontal flex of vertical columns. A proper fix needs:

1. A new `layoutMode?: 'cascade' | 'pyramid'` prop on `GenericCascadeBoard`, or a separate `PyramidBoard` component, that renders rows horizontally with a vertical row offset (so adjacent rows visually overlap by ~half a card height to form the triangle).
2. Verification that pair-click interaction (Pyramid is "click two cards summing to 13", not cascade-drag) lands on the correct cards at the new positions.
3. A small mobile-first sizing pass since 7 cards × `--card-width` won't fit at 375 vw without a custom Pyramid clamp.

Bigger refactor than fits in a daily QA cycle. Shipping the descoping note as the actionable backlog entry; will revisit in a dedicated session.
