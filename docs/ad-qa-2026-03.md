## Policy Risk Assessment

### Ads Near Interactive Controls

**GameShell sidebar (desktop):**
- Ads are in a separate column to the right of the game area, separated by a border
- Fixed dimensions (300x250, 300x600) with container wrappers prevent overflow
- Gated behind 2+ games played — not present on first visit
- **Risk: LOW.** Sidebar is physically separated from game controls. No accidental click path.

**Content pages:**
- All content-page ads are placed between article sections, never inside interactive elements
- No ads are positioned adjacent to buttons, links, or form controls within the same visual block
- **Risk: LOW.** Standard content interstitial pattern.

### Ads Near Draggable Cards

- The GameShell's right sidebar ads are outside the game board container
- The game board is in a centered flex container; the sidebar is a separate flex child
- Cards cannot be dragged into the sidebar area — drop targets are within the board
- **Risk: LOW.** No overlap between drag surfaces and ad surfaces.

### Accidental Click Risk

- No ads overlay or float above content
- No sticky/fixed-position ads
- No ads that expand or animate into content areas
- No interstitial or popup ad formats
- All ads use standard `<ins class="adsbygoogle">` elements with `overflow: hidden`
- **Risk: LOW.**

### Excessive Density

- Strategy page has 6 ad placements across ~900 lines of content — roughly one ad per 150 lines
- Spider strategy, Spider how-to-play, Baker's Game strategy, and Eight Off strategy each have 5 placements
- These are long-form content pages (2000+ words), so the density is acceptable
- Shorter content pages (easy games, hard games, hints explained) have 1-2 placements — appropriate
- **Risk: LOW to MEDIUM.** The 6-placement pages are borderline. Content length justifies it, but if AdSense flags density issues, strategy pages would be the first place to reduce.

### Mobile Crowding

- All content-page ads use `data-full-width-responsive="true"` — fills container width on mobile
- GameShell sidebar ads are `hidden lg:flex` — hidden on mobile entirely
- No ads are forced above the fold on mobile content pages (content comes first)
- The `my-0` spacing on some pages means ads are flush with adjacent content — could feel tight on small screens
- **Risk: LOW to MEDIUM.** The `my-0` placements on history, probability, game-11982, cheat-sheet, easy/hard games, and is-every-game-winnable pages have zero vertical margin. On mobile, this can make ads feel crowded against adjacent text. Switching to `my-4` would improve readability without losing viewability.

### Incentivized Clicks

- No text near any ad unit that could be interpreted as encouraging clicks
- No "click here" or "support us" language near ads
- No ads styled to look like content or navigation
- **Risk: NONE.**

### Layout Shift (CLS)

- GameShell sidebar: fixed-dimension containers (300x250, 300x600) — no CLS
- Content pages: responsive auto-format ads have no reserved height — they collapse to 0 before loading, then expand
- The `<div className="ad-container overflow-hidden">` wrapper does not set a min-height
- **Risk: MEDIUM.** Responsive auto-format ads on content pages will cause layout shift when they load. This is the single most impactful ad-related CLS issue. Adding a `min-height` to the ad container would reserve space and reduce CLS.

## Recommendations

### Ship Now

1. **Add min-height to ad containers on content pages.** The AdUnit component's wrapper div should reserve vertical space to prevent CLS. A `min-height: 90px` (the minimum height for a responsive AdSense unit) would significantly reduce layout shift on content pages.

2. **Remove dead AdUnit imports.** `tips/page.tsx` and `glossary/page.tsx` both import AdUnit but never render it. Remove the unused imports.

3. **Increase spacing on zero-margin ad placements.** Change `my-0` to `my-4` on the following pages to improve mobile readability:
   - history/page.tsx (4 placements)
   - freecell-probability/page.tsx (3 placements)
   - freecell-game-11982/page.tsx (2 placements)
   - freecell-cheat-sheet/page.tsx (3 placements)
   - easy-freecell-games/page.tsx (1 placement)
   - hard-freecell-games/page.tsx (1 placement)
   - is-every-freecell-game-winnable/page.tsx (1 placement)
   - freecell-world-records/page.tsx (2 placements)

### Do Not Ship

- No changes to the GameShell sidebar — it's well-implemented
- No new ad placements on zero-ad pages — not needed at current traffic
- No ad refresh or video ad integration
- No Freestar/Mediavine SDK integration at this time
