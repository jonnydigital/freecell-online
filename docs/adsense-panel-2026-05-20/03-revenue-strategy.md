## 2. 2026 RPM Benchmarks — Casual-Game / Card-Game Publishers

### 2.1 The honest baseline

Game sites are one of the lowest-RPM categories in web publishing. The relevant data points, sourced from 2025–2026:

**AdSense (raw, no optimization):**
- Entertainment/gaming niche: **$1–3 RPM** is the documented range for casual games and card games. [MonetizeMore, "Best AdSense Alternatives for H5 Games"](https://www.monetizemore.com/blog/best-adsense-alternatives-for-h5-game/)
- Cross-confirmed: the competitive-strategy.md in this repo pegs AdSense at "$1–3 RPM" for this niche, consistent with the above.
- For context, sports sites earn $9–18 RPM; finance earns $10–50 RPM. Solitaire/FreeCell is nowhere near those.

**The Online Solitaire benchmark (best available real-world data point):**
- online-solitaire.com: ~1M monthly visitors, ~4M game plays/month, **$10,000 MRR** from ads via Freestar. [Indie Hackers](https://www.indiehackers.com/post/how-i-grew-a-simple-solitaire-game-to-10k-mrr-28e352c308)
- Implied site-wide RPM: $10,000 revenue / 1,000,000 visits × 1,000 = **~$10 RPM**
- This is with a premium header-bidding stack (Freestar), ad refresh during long gameplay sessions, and viewability optimization (Freestar case study: 2000% revenue increase vs. baseline, CPMs up 177%). [Freestar case study](https://freestar.com/case-studies/online-solitaire/)
- Before Freestar optimization, the site earned ~$500–$1,500/month at ~200K–400K visits. That implies a pre-optimization RPM of roughly **$1.50–$5**.

**The solitaired.com sanity check:**
- ~23M annual visits (Similarweb data), reportedly $200K+/year revenue (referenced in this repo's competitive-strategy.md)
- $200,000 / 23,000,000 visits × 1,000 = **$8.70 RPM site-wide**
- This is a header-bidding optimized, mature site. It represents the realistic ceiling for a well-run solitaire property at scale.
- At current traffic (~5K–15K monthly visits), this site cannot achieve $8.70 RPM — premium networks either won't accept low-traffic sites or won't deliver premium fill rates on thin inventory.

**The RPM ladder in this niche:**

| Stack | Expected RPM | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Raw AdSense | $1.00 – $2.50 | Verified across gaming/entertainment publishers |
| AdSense + light optimization | $2.00 – $4.00 | Better ad placement, 3 units |
| Mediavine Journey | $5.00 – $12.00 | Real publisher data: $12.69 avg in one 90-day report; lower for gaming vs. recipe niches |
| Freestar / premium header bidding | $8.00 – $20.00 | online-solitaire.com ~$10 RPM; requires 1M+ PV minimum for Freestar |
| Freestar + video + refresh | $15.00 – $30.00 | Online sessions of 5+ min with slot refresh; ceiling for this niche at scale |

**Important caveat on Mediavine Journey RPM:** The $12–19 RPMs cited in Journey publisher reports come primarily from recipe, lifestyle, and food bloggers — not gaming sites. Game-site RPMs through Journey are likely lower, estimated **$5–10** range, because advertiser demand is thinner for gaming audiences vs. purchase-intent content audiences. This is an assumption; no public Journey gaming-specific RPM data was found.

## 6. Recommendation

**Execute Scenario B as the immediate default, with a defined upgrade trigger to Scenario C.**

### Immediate actions (this week)

1. **Apply to Mediavine Journey for solitairestack.com, playklondikeonline.com, and playspidersolitaireonline.com.** The 1,000-session threshold means two of the three sites may already qualify. Application is free and takes days. Stop waiting for AdSense on these sites — the AdSense blocking on "low value content" is telling you something real: those sites don't have enough content quality to satisfy Google's bar. Mediavine Journey's bar is lower and their RPM for this niche is still 3–5x AdSense.

2. **Keep AdSense on playfreecellonline.com — for now.** It's the only approved site. Do not abandon a guaranteed income stream (even small) without a direct replacement live and tested.

3. **Set a Scenario C upgrade trigger:** If Mediavine Journey delivers $5+ RPM on any of the three sites within 60 days, apply Journey to playfreecellonline.com as well and drop AdSense. If Journey RPM for game sites proves lower than $4, reconsider.

### Why not Scenario A

Waiting for AdSense approval on three sites with documented "low value content" blocks is a time-sink with zero upside. AdSense's 2026 rejection criteria are tighter than ever — AI-generated content suspicion, thin content, "information gain" requirements. The sites that are blocked need either (a) substantial content upgrades to clear AdSense's bar, or (b) a different network that doesn't require AdSense-level content gates. Mediavine Journey is that alternative.

### Why not pure Scenario C immediately

Dropping AdSense on the one approved site before confirming Journey delivers equivalent or better revenue for a game site is unnecessary risk. There is no cost to running Scenario B — AdSense on one site, Journey on three — while gathering 60 days of real data.

### The real multiplier: traffic

All three scenarios produce modest revenue at current traffic levels. The difference between earning $500/month and $5,000/month in this network is **traffic, not ad network choice.** The embeddable widget strategy (documented in competitive-strategy.md), indexed deal pages, and deploying the network are the revenue levers — the ad network optimization is a coefficient on traffic, not a substitute for it.

At 100K monthly visits with a $10 RPM stack: $1,000/month.
At 1M monthly visits with a $10 RPM stack: $10,000/month.

That is the range available in this niche. The ceiling (solitaired.com at $200K/year) requires 23M visits and a mature, optimized ad stack — a multi-year build.

## 7. The Solitaired.com Sanity Check

Is $8.70 site-wide RPM a realistic ceiling for this niche?

**Short answer: Yes, roughly. The actual ceiling with video ads and refresh may be $15–20 RPM site-wide, but that requires infrastructure and traffic scale not reachable in Year 1.**

**The math:**
- solitaired.com: ~23M annual visits, $200K/year implied revenue
- $200,000 / 23,000,000 × 1,000 = **$8.70 site-wide RPM**
- online-solitaire.com: ~1M visits/month, $10K/month = **$10.00 site-wide RPM** (with Freestar optimized)
- 123freecell.com: ~795K/month, enterprise ad stack (Prebid + Amazon TAM + video) — revenue not public but architecture suggests $15–25 RPM is possible

**What drives the RPM in this niche:**
1. Long session durations (solitaire sessions average 5–19 minutes per Similarweb data on solitaired.com showing 28:45 average visit duration) → many ad impressions per user
2. Slot refresh: a 30-second refresh during a 20-minute session = 40 impressions per slot per user, not 1
3. Video ads: 123freecell runs video; this alone adds $5–10 RPM incremental
4. Header bidding: AdSense earns ~$1–2 CPM on gaming; header bidding pulls $4–8 CPM from the same inventory
5. Ad blocker recovery: ~30% of gaming audiences use blockers; recovery tech recovers 15–20% of that

**Without video and slot refresh (playfreecellonline.com's current situation):**
- AdSense-only ceiling: ~$2–3 RPM
- Premium display-only (no video, no refresh): ~$5–8 RPM
- With video + refresh (future state): ~$12–20 RPM

**The $8.70 RPM is achievable but not in Year 1.** In Year 1 at sub-100K monthly visits, premium networks either won't accept you (Freestar at 1M PV minimum) or won't deliver full fill rates on thin inventory. Realistic Year-1 achievable RPM with Mediavine Journey: **$5–10** (with the caveat that game-niche Journey RPMs are unconfirmed — see assumption log).
